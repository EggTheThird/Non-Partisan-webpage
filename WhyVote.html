Trump's voter demographic is comprised mainly of white, middle aged men. College educated Americans tend to side more with Clinton, as do women and racial minorities. Stein ad Johnson offer alternatives to the two mainstream candidates, Stein targeting the environmentally conscious and Johnson those who reject the pervasiveness of our current federal government.
