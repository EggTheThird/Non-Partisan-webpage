Election Background

Donald Trump and Hillary Clinton are the two primary candidates in the 2016 presidential election, with Jill Stein and Gary Johnson running as third party candidates. Donald Trump is running for the republican party, Clinton for the democratic party, Johnson as a libertarian, and Stein for the Green party.
